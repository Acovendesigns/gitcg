OUR STORY

Coven-Goldman Associates is a full service commercial real estate firm. We specialize in real estate development, real estate brokerage and property management. Located in Cleveland, Ohio, Coven-Goldman Associates and related entities own and manage over 1,500,000 square feet of single-story office and warehouse buildings. These buildings are situated in different suburban areas in and around Greater Cleveland. Each building provides excellent highway access and both functional and attractive space. Most buildings have been developed within the last 30 years.

Availability of space is constantly changing. To find available Coven-Goldman space, go to our Available Properties page or give us a call at (216) 464-2140, either Marcia Coven, Larry Coven or Brad Coven, SIOR will be happy to provide you with an update on our available properties. Each of our properties has individual floor plans for each suite and/or building. Go to our Buildings page and choose a building to see site plans and floor plans for available spaces.
Our buildings have been designed to accommodate office and warehouse tenants in the 2,000 square foot to 20,000 square foot range, although smaller and larger units do occasionally become available. Some of our current tenants include Allen-Bradley Co., Dal-Tile, Citi Financial, Uddeholm Steel, Orkin, Invacare, Motorola, Goodyear, Ameritech, Ryan Homes and many other national and local companies.

Coven-Goldman Associates has been involved in industrial and commercial real estate since the 1950's. Today, Coven-Goldman Associates provides leasing, property and asset management, acquisition, development, build-to-suit and real estate brokerage services

OUR SERVICES

BROKERAGE

Coven Goldman Associates is a real estate brokerage firm based in Cleveland, Ohio, specializing in the representation of landlords, tenants and users of office and industrial real estate. Since the 1950’s, Coven Goldman Associates has leased and sold millions of square feet of commercial space in and around the Greater Cleveland area and around the country.

The brokers at Coven Goldman Associates are true professionals with the ability to handle both landlord and tenant representation competently, creatively, and with integrity. The firm is constantly aware of changing market trends and conditions. Coven Goldman Associates has been noted for expertise in areas such as construction management, office rehabbing, and land sales and land leases. The company has been instrumental in obtaining tax incentives for clients and has assisted with lease renewal and expansion negotiations and site selection for many companies. Additionally, Coven Goldman Associates has completed many build-to-suit projects. Coven Goldman Associates provides their clients with the highest level of service and results. Finally, a principal at Coven Goldman, Bradley Coven, SIOR is affiliated with The National Association of Realtors and is an Individual Member of The Society of Industrial and Office REALTORS.

MANAGEMENT

Ruma Management is the Cleveland Commercial Property Management Division of Coven-Goldman Associates. We provide commercial management services for a variety of property types. Whether your building provides a home to your company or is part of an investment portfolio, a poorly managed building will make even the most promising asset fail to meet owner expectations. RUMA Management will help your property live up to its full potential.

RUMA Management’s team has first-hand experience with the issues and opportunities in residential and commercial properties. We understand the economic environment and are well-equipped to maintain and enhance the value of each property we manage. Procuring tenants and maintaining their satisfaction are the foundation of a successful building. Our specialty is managing your commercial assets to maximize bottom line profits, keep tenants, and minimize your investment risk.

Whether your property is Class "A" or Class "C", large or small, we can handle it. We recognize that different types of properties need to be managed in different ways. Each commercial property can and should be managed in a way that maximizes the properties potential, holds to the owner's standard in terms of care and maintenance, and gets the appropriate attention in all aspects of property management including billing, maintenance, tenant negotiations, vendor relations, security, collections, follow-up and service.
